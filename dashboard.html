Caveats.
  • V100-class only — magnitudes & crossover-n are bandwidth-dependent (PR author: 2.69× for the same dense model on a DGX Spark).
  • Throughput only — greedy DFlash isn't bit-identical under 2-GPU tensor-split (FP reduction order + MoE routing); a single-GPU n=1 run was 40/40 identical, so it's a placement artifact, not a lossy rule. Output quality not evaluated.
  • Fast-dense-4B is confounded (single-GPU, ~15 pp lower acceptance, self-converted drafter) — suggestive, not proof.
  • Non-thinking is the primary regime; thinking traces draft better, so these are a lower bound for reasoning-heavy use.
